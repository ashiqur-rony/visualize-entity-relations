A visual analysis of textual data to identify persons of interest based on their activities.
Ashiqur Rahman
Department of Computer Science
Northern Illinois University

The user can perform the following actions to explore the visualization:

  • Choosing whether to show WHAT (activity) linked to the entities.
  • Hovering on an item will highlight the connections recursively.
  • Clicking on any of the items will display the relevant text sources and scroll to that.
